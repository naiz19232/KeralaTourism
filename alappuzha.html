About Alappuzha

Often called the "Venice of the East," Alappuzha is famous for its backwaters, houseboats, and the annual Nehru Trophy Boat Race.

Tourist Attractions

  • Alappuzha Beach
  • Nehru Trophy Boat Race
  • Vembanad Lake
  • Marari Beach

Culture

Alappuzha's culture is deeply influenced by the backwaters and the traditional boat races. It's also known for its coir industry and cultural festivals.
