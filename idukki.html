About Idukki

Idukki is famous for its stunning hill stations, wildlife sanctuaries, and the Idukki Dam, one of the largest arch dams in Asia.

Tourist Attractions

  • Idukki Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Idukki Arch Dam
  • Thekkady
  • Munnar

Culture

Idukki's culture is influenced by the diverse communities living in the region. It's known for its traditional dance forms and vibrant festivals.
