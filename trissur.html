Culture

Thrissur is often referred to as the "Cultural Capital of Kerala" and is known for its rich cultural traditions, classical art forms, and historic significance.

Festivals

Thrissur Pooram, a grand temple festival, is one of the most famous festivals celebrated in Thrissur. It features magnificent elephant processions and cultural performances.

Tourist Attractions

  • Vadakkunnathan Temple
  • Thrissur Zoo
  • Athirappilly Waterfalls
  • Vilangan Kunnu

Food Spots

Thrissur is known for its delicious traditional Kerala cuisine. Don't miss out on trying local specialties like "Sadya," "Parotta," and "Elaneer Payasam."
