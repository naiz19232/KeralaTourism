Educational Institutions

Malappuram is known for its educational institutions, including the renowned Kottakkal Arya Vaidya Sala, which is a leading Ayurvedic treatment center.

Historic Sites

Malappuram boasts several historic sites, including Kottakkunnu, which offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape, and the historic Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary.

Famous Ayurvedic Center

Malappuram is home to the renowned Kottakkal Arya Vaidya Sala, a center known for traditional Ayurvedic treatments and therapies.

Culinary Delights

Malappuram offers a variety of culinary delights. Be sure to savor local specialties like Malabar Biryani and authentic Kerala cuisine in the region.
