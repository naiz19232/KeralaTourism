About Trivandrum

Trivandrum, officially known as Thiruvananthapuram, is the capital city of the Indian state of Kerala. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful beaches, historic monuments, and lush green landscapes.

Tourist Attractions

  • Padmanabhaswamy Temple
  • Kovalam Beach
  • Napier Museum
  • Poovar Island

Culture

Trivandrum is a vibrant cultural center, known for its classical dance forms like Kathakali and traditional music. The city is also famous for its religious festivals and rituals.
