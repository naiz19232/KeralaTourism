About Kasaragod

Kasaragod is a beautiful district located in the northern part of Kerala, known for its serene beaches, historic forts, and cultural diversity.

Tourist Attractions

  • Bekal Fort
  • Kappil Beach
  • Ranipuram
  • Madhur Temple

Climate

Kasaragod enjoys a tropical monsoon climate with heavy rainfall during the monsoon season. The weather is humid and pleasant, making it a great year-round destination.

Geography

The district is characterized by its picturesque landscapes, including hills, rivers, and backwaters. The Western Ghats run through the eastern part of Kasaragod, adding to its natural beauty.

Culture

Kasaragod is a melting pot of cultures, with influences from Karnataka and the Tulu-speaking community. The region celebrates various festivals and cultural events.

Food Spots

Kasaragod offers a unique culinary experience with a blend of Kerala and coastal Karnataka cuisines. Don't miss trying local dishes like Neer Dosa, Fish Curry, and Jackfruit Halwa.
