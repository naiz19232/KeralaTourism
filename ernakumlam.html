About Ernakulam

Ernakulam, or Cochin, is a major commercial and industrial hub. It's known for its diverse culture, Fort Kochi, and the Chinese fishing nets.

Tourist Attractions

  • Fort Kochi
  • Chinese Fishing Nets
  • Mattancherry Palace
  • Jew Town

Culture

Ernakulam has a vibrant and diverse culture, influenced by various communities that call it home. The city is known for its historic landmarks and cultural festivals.
