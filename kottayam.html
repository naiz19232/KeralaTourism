About Kottayam

Kottayam is known for its rubber plantations, beautiful landscapes, and cultural heritage. It's also the center of the Syrian Christian community in Kerala.

Tourist Attractions

  • Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary
  • Ettumanoor Mahadeva Temple
  • Vembanad Backwaters
  • St. Mary's Knanaya Church

Culture

Kottayam boasts a rich cultural heritage and is the center of the Syrian Christian community in Kerala. It's known for its churches, religious festivals, and cultural traditions.
