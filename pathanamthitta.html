About Pathanamthitta

Pathanamthitta is known for its lush green landscapes and pilgrimage sites, including Sabarimala, a major Hindu pilgrimage destination.

Tourist Attractions

  • Sabarimala Temple
  • Perunthenaruvi Waterfall
  • Aranmula Parthasarathy Temple
  • Pandavan Para

Culture

Pathanamthitta has a rich cultural heritage, and it is closely associated with the traditions and rituals of Sabarimala. The annual Sabarimala pilgrimage is a significant cultural event in the region.
